Our Banquet and Catering Services

Situated at the base of the mountains in North Vancouver, amidst a beautiful west coast forest of firs and cedars, overlooking one of BC's great golf courses, Asiane delivers a "getaway" experience that is only 15 minutes from downtown Vancouver.

If you are organizing a wedding, a business meeting, an anniversary, or a Celebration of Life, we have a team in place that will make the entire process as stress free as it can be.

We have audio-visual equipment, breakout rooms, modern furnishings, and a beautiful patio overlooking the golf course if you need to get out and breathe in the mountain air and the ocean breezes. Give us a call and let us guide you through the process of organizing an unforgettable experience for your guests. We also specialize in custom golf tournaments and weddings.
